Resort Information

Enjoy Conrad Bora Bora Nui’s blissful location and services on one of the most beautiful Pacific islands

Island Host

To make your visit as stress-free as possible, your dedicated Island Host will take care of every aspect of your stay.

We provide an array of services to all our guests such as packing and unpacking, welcome service, resort tours, private cocktail hour, morning beverage delivery, turndown service, laundry and many more.

Hilton Cleanstay

We are committed to providing a safe and hospitable environment for all who visit our island. In consultation with global and local public health authorities, we have taken additional measures to make our cleaning and hygiene protocols even more rigorous

Entry & movement conditions

Before your stay, we recommend visiting Tahiti Tourism board’s website for the most up-to-date official information related to Covid-19 in French Polynesia.
